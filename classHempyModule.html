Supports monitoring two Hempy buckets Runs autonomously on an Arduino Nano RF and communicates wirelessly with the main module

Passing ModuleSettings members as references: Changes get written back to ModuleSettings and saved to EEPROM. (uint8_t *)(((uint8_t *)&ModuleSettings) + offsetof(Settings, VARIABLENAME))

Subscribing to the 5 sec refresh queue: Calls the refresh_FiveSec() method
